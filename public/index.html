Emerald Waters Plant

1729 Ocean Bluff Road, Brownsmead OR

The Emerald Waters Plant is a tidal field located at the mouth of the Columbia River, several miles west of Vancouver, Washington. Emerald Waters' first unit began commercial service in 1994, and its second unit followed in 1998.

Turbine Name: 3SA.01

Smith Energy Complex

328 Energy Way, Snowflake, AZ

The Jingleheimer Smith Jr. Energy Complex, south of Snowflake, AZ, includes five large solar fields that generate 1,084 MW. The site began commercial operation in 2001, with additions in 2002 and 2011.

Turbine Name: W7B.01

King County Farm

807 Green Field Rd, Titonka, IA

The 520-megawatt King County Wind Farm consists of five fields of wind turbine units, and is part of the Tesla Energy Complex near Saphire Lake, Iowa. The plant began operation in 2000 with an addition in 2009.
